Help

            
          

          

            

              This database summarizes study-level variables extracted for the review paper:
              The Evolution of Data-Driven Management Zone Delineation: A Systematic Review.
            

            

              
Workflow group (e.g., A1, D5)

              

                These are workflow group codes defined in the paper (Dimension 2: Zoning Mechanism Subsection). A paper may belong to multiple workflow groups.
                Use the filter to select papers that contain at least one selected workflow group.
              

            

            

              
Validation tier (e.g., VAL_TIER_C)

              

                Validation tiers are defined in the paper (Validation and Evaluation Approaches Subsection). A paper may report multiple tiers.
              

            

            

              For definitions and examples of all codes (workflow groups, validation tiers, etc.), please refer to the paper.
